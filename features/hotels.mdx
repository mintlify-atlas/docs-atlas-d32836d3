## Search for hotels

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Hotels page">
    Open your trip and click **Hotels** in the navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter destination">
    Type the city or area where you need accommodation (e.g., "Paris, France").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set dates">
    Select check-in and check-out dates.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Specify guests">
    Enter number of guests and rooms needed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search">
    Click **Search Hotels** to see results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Hotel search uses mock data for display and UI testing. Integration with booking APIs is in progress.
</Note>

## Filter and sort results

Refine your search using the filters panel:

### Price range
- Move the slider to set minimum and maximum price per night
- Prices shown in your selected currency

### Star rating
- Filter by hotel classification (1-5 stars)
- Select multiple star levels

### Amenities
- WiFi, Pool, Gym, Parking
- Breakfast included, Spa, Restaurant
- Check amenities to match your needs

### Distance from center
- Filter hotels by proximity to city center
- Useful for prioritizing location convenience

<Tip>
  Use the "Best Pick" indicator to identify hotels with the best combination of price, rating, and location.
</Tip>

## Hotel result cards

Each hotel displays:

- **Hotel name and area**
- **Star rating** - Classification from 1-5 stars
- **Guest review score** - Average rating from reviews
- **Review count** - Number of reviews
- **Price per night** - Room rate for your dates
- **Room type** - Standard, Deluxe, Suite, etc.
- **Amenities** - Icons for WiFi, Pool, Gym, etc.
- **Distance to center** - How far from city center
- **Best Pick badge** - Highlighted recommendations

### View details

Click on a hotel card to see:
- Full amenity list
- Room photos and descriptions
- Cancellation policy
- Guest reviews
- Location map

## Add hotels to trip

Save promising options for later comparison:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse results">
    Review the hotel list and identify candidates.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Add to Trip">
    On the hotel card, click **Add to Trip**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm selection">
    The hotel saves to your trip's accommodations list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compare options">
    View all saved hotels in the trip overview to make your final decision.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Track hotel bookings

After booking externally:

1. Navigate to **Finance** page
2. Add the hotel cost as an expense in the "Hotels" category
3. Use the **Hotel Split** allocator to spread cost across nights
4. Add booking confirmation details to itinerary notes

<Warning>
  TripLoom displays hotel search results but does not process payments directly. Book through the hotel's website or third-party booking platforms.
</Warning>
